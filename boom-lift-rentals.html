Where boom lifts are used

Typical boom lift applications on site

Boom lifts are chosen wherever teams need repeat access to elevated positions with a mix of vertical and horizontal movement, without building scaffold each time.

Construction & finishing works

Use boom lifts to reach façade panels, steel connections, roof edges and hard-to-reach junctions during construction and finishing stages. The platform lets workers carry tools and hardware while staying in a controlled working position.

  • Façade cladding and glazing installation
  • Roof perimeter and soffit work
  • Bridges, flyovers and elevated structures

Industrial & plant maintenance

In industrial facilities and warehouses, boom lifts give access to equipment, pipe racks, conveyors and structural members. This reduces time spent erecting temporary platforms for short-duration jobs.

  • Shutdown and turnaround activities
  • Inspection of high-level equipment
  • Cleaning and corrosion repairs

Lighting, utilities & signage

Outdoor boom lifts support teams working on street lighting, high-mounted signage and communication equipment, allowing quick repositioning along roads and corridors.

Boom lift options

Choose the boom configuration that suits your site

Different boom lift designs solve different access challenges. You can rent one type for a focused task or combine multiple models on complex projects.

Articulated booms

Reach up and over obstacles

Articulated boom lifts have multiple joints that allow the platform to rise, bend and reach over obstacles such as canopies, pipe racks or building projections.

Ideal for: congested sites & complex geometry

Telescopic booms

Long, straight horizontal outreach

Telescopic booms extend in a straight line, providing maximum outreach where you have open ground or clear approach paths and need to work further away from the base.

Ideal for: open sites & long spans

Electric & hybrid booms

Low-noise access for clean environments

Electric and hybrid boom lifts help you work inside factories, warehouses and finished commercial spaces with reduced emissions and quieter operation.

Ideal for: indoor & low-emission zones
